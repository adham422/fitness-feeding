Nutrition tips

Eating foods in their natural form and avoiding processed or packaged foods as much as possible is an ideal choice for healthy eating. For example, eating a whole turkey is better than frozen turkey meatballs. Eating grapes is also better than packaged fruit. Staying away from processed foods and avoiding consuming large amounts of calories, saturated fats, sugar and salt is an ideal option.
