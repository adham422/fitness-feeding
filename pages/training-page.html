Exercise Cardio

Cardio is defined as exercise aimed at reducing weight and getting rid of body fat, in addition to helping to highlight muscles in a short period time. In addition, it speeds up the heartbeat and breathing, which leads to improved breathing for those who practice it. These exercises will also enhance the level of oxygen in the blood, which leads to improved health in general.
